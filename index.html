About Me

Hey there, fellow block enthusiasts! I'm TheDevMango, a passionate Minecraft Mod, Datapack, and Resourcepack Developer. My journey in the blocky universe began with a fascination for how things works and a desire to add my own twist to the game.

I specialize in creating unique and engaging content that enhances gameplay, whether it's through new crafting recipes, custom mechanics, or stunning visual overhauls. My goal is always to deliver high-quality, bug-free, and fun additions that seamlessly integrate into your Minecraft adventures.

When I'm not coding, you can probably find me exploring new biomes, building elaborate redstone contraptions, or brainstorming the next big idea for a datapack!

I am a Master in Datapack and Resourcepack Development but im Learning Fabric Mod Development. I will release Mods Soon too.

I am also a Passionate Python, JS and Godot Developer.

Craftables Datapack

The Craftables Datapack adds Some Crafting Recipes that were previosly only avaliable through Loot and Creative. Such as Horse Armor, NameTag, Ore Blocks and more. It also has more stuff like being able to Craft Invisible Item and Glow Item Frames and Armor Stands spawning with Arms by default.
